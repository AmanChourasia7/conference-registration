Our History

Since its inception in 2010, the International Tech Conference has grown to become one of the premier technology events in the world. What started as a small gathering of 200 tech enthusiasts has evolved into a global phenomenon attracting thousands of participants annually.

Over the years, we've hosted some of the most influential figures in technology, facilitated countless partnerships, and witnessed the launch of groundbreaking innovations. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us a trusted platform for knowledge sharing and networking in the tech community.
