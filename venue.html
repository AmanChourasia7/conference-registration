Things to Do in San Francisco

Golden Gate Bridge

Iconic suspension bridge offering stunning views. Perfect for photos and walks.

Fisherman's Wharf

Waterfront area with seafood restaurants, shops, and sea lion viewing.

Alcatraz Island

Historic federal penitentiary. Tours available (book in advance).

Cable Cars

Historic cable car rides through the city's steep hills. A San Francisco must-do!

Chinatown

Vibrant neighborhood with authentic restaurants, shops, and cultural attractions.

Tech Museums

Exploratorium and California Academy of Sciences offer fascinating exhibits.
