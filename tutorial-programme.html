Tutorial Programme

Friday, 8 January 2027 (Tutorial Day 1)

08:00-09:00 Breakfast & Registration
09:00-09:45 Dr. Ananya Sharma
Foundations of Optimization in Machine Learning
Introduction to optimization problems and their role in modern ML systems.
09:45-10:30 Prof. Vivek Singh
Convex Optimization and Applications
Core principles of convexity and efficient solution techniques.
10:30-11:15 Dr. Neha Gupta
Gradient Descent and Variants
Analysis of gradient-based optimization methods in practice.
11:15-12:00 Prof. Arjun Mehta
Optimization Challenges in Deep Learning
Understanding non-convex landscapes and training stability.
12:00-13:30 Lunch Break
13:30-14:15 Dr. Sneha Iyer
Stochastic Optimization Techniques
Mini-batch methods and stochastic approximations.
14:15-15:00 Prof. Karan Verma
Optimization in Reinforcement Learning
Policy optimization and value-based methods.
15:00-15:45 Dr. Pooja Nair
Large-Scale Optimization Systems
Scalable optimization for distributed environments.
15:45-16:30 Prof. Rohit Das
Second-Order Methods and Acceleration
Advanced optimization techniques and convergence speedups.
16:30-17:00 Discussion & Problem Solving Competition
Interactive session with applied optimization challenges.
17:00 onwards Dinner

Saturday, 9 January 2027 (Tutorial Day 2)

08:00-09:00 Breakfast
09:00-09:45 Dr. Ritu Malhotra
Optimization in Probabilistic Models
Inference and optimization in probabilistic frameworks.
09:45-10:30 Prof. Aditya Banerjee
Regularization and Generalization
Balancing model complexity and performance.
10:30-11:15 Dr. Kunal Roy
Optimization for Computer Vision
Applications in image recognition and vision tasks.
11:15-12:00 Prof. Meera Iyer
Optimization in Natural Language Processing
Training large-scale language models efficiently.
12:00-13:30 Lunch Break
13:30-14:15 Dr. Sandeep Kulkarni
Adaptive Optimization Algorithms
Adam, RMSProp, and modern adaptive methods.
14:15-15:00 Prof. Nikhil Srivastava
Optimization Theory and Guarantees
Convergence theory and performance bounds.
15:00-15:45 Dr. Aditi Chatterjee
Optimization in Federated Learning
Decentralized optimization across distributed devices.
15:45-16:30 Panel Discussion
Future Directions in Optimization and Machine Learning
16:30-17:00 Closing Remarks
