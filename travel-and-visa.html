Travel & Visa Information

Welcome to OML 2027 at the Indian Institute of Technology Patna. This page provides practical guidance for domestic and international delegates on how to reach the venue, travel within the host city, and prepare visa documentation.

Important: Please plan your travel early and allow sufficient time for visa processing, airport transfers, and local transportation. For accommodation support, visit the Accommodation page.

Arrival by Air

The nearest airport is Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport (PAT) in Patna, approximately 18 km from IIT Patna. The airport connects to major Indian cities including Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bengaluru, and Hyderabad.

  • Taxi or app-based ride: 35–45 minutes.
  • Prepaid airport taxi counters are available inside the arrival terminal.
  • Delegate shuttle service details will be published closer to the conference dates.

Arrival by Train

Patna Junction (PNBE) and Rajendra Nagar Terminal (RJPB) are the main railway stations serving Patna. Both stations are well connected by trains from all parts of India.

  • Travel time from the station to IIT Patna is 25–35 minutes by taxi.
  • Book train tickets early through the IRCTC website or through authorized agents.
  • Auto-rickshaws and prepaid taxis are available at both stations.

Arrival by Road

Patna is linked by national highways and regular bus services from nearby cities such as Varanasi, Ranchi, and Kolkata.

  • Private cars and taxis can access IIT Patna via Bailey Road and NH19.
  • There are also state-run and private bus services to Patna central bus terminals.

Local Transport

Local travel options include taxis, app-based ride services, auto-rickshaws, and campus shuttle buses.

  • Ask your hotel for reliable local travel recommendations.
  • If you arrive at the airport or station, choose prepaid taxis for safer and fixed fares.
  • Allow extra time during peak traffic hours in Patna.

Visa Guidance for International Delegates

International participants should begin visa application procedures well before travel. IIT Patna and conference organizers cannot issue visas, but we can provide invitation letters for registered delegates.

Visa Types

  • e-Visa: India offers e-Tourist, e-Business, and e-Medical visas. Apply through the official Indian government visa portal.
  • Regular Visa: Apply at your nearest Indian embassy or consulate if you require a sticker visa or have special travel needs.

Required Documents

  • Passport valid for at least six months from the date of arrival.
  • Passport-size photograph and scanned passport biodata page.
  • Confirmed return or onward travel itinerary.
  • Conference invitation letter (available after registration).
  • Any additional documentation requested by your consulate.

Conference Logistics

Venue Access

IIT Patna is located in Bihta, approximately 40 kilometres from central Patna. The conference venue is LH001 and LH004 inside the IIT Patna campus. Please carry a valid ID and your conference registration details for campus entry.
