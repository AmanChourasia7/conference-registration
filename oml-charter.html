OML Charter

This charter defines the vision, principles, and expectations for the Optimization and Machine Learning (OML) conference series. It is designed to support researchers, students, and industry practitioners.

1. Mission

The mission of OML is to foster innovation in optimization and machine learning by creating an inclusive forum for collaboration, discovery, and responsible application.

2. Vision

  • Support rigorous research in optimization, learning algorithms, and AI systems.
  • Connect global researchers through open, accessible conference programs.
  • Encourage reproducibility, open science, and ethical practices.

3. Principles

  1. Scientific excellence — foster high-quality research and fair peer review.
  2. Openness — encourage transparency, reproducibility, and clear communication.
  3. Inclusivity — promote diversity, respect, and equal opportunity.
  4. Integrity — uphold ethical conduct in research and collaboration.

4. Scope

  • Optimization theory, algorithms, and practical methods.
  • Machine learning models, training, evaluation, and deployment.
  • Interdisciplinary applications across AI, data science, operations research, and engineering.

5. Governance

The OML series is guided by a programme committee and organising committee responsible for technical direction, speaker selection, and maintaining quality.

6. Community

  • Provide mentorship and support for students and early career researchers.
  • Encourage collaboration between academia, industry, and government.
  • Share best practices for open research and reproducibility.
