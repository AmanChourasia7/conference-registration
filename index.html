OML 2027

Optimization and Machine Learning (OML) is a rapidly developing field spanning applied mathematics, computer science, and artificial intelligence. The field focuses on designing efficient algorithms for optimization problems and leveraging data-driven models for learning, prediction, and decision-making.

OML 2027 is the inaugural conference on theoretical and practical aspects of optimization, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. The conference will feature a tutorial programme, invited talks, contributed talks, and a poster session. In addition, there will be a rump session with short informal presentations.
