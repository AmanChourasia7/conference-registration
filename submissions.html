Submission

Submit your research paper for consideration at Conference 2026.

  • Submit the manuscript in PDF format (.pdf)
  • Ensure the file size does not exceed 10 MB
  • Prepare a paper of 6 to 8 pages including references
  • Follow the OML conference template for formatting
  • Remove all author information to ensure blind review
  • Write the paper in English
  • Submit before April 1, 2026 at 11:59 PM EST
  • Ensure the work is original and has not been previously published
