PAPER SUBMISSION

Submit Your Paper
Submit your research paper for consideration at Conference 2026

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

  • File Format: PDF only (.pdf)
  • File Size: Maximum 10 MB
  • Paper Length: 6-8 pages including references
  • Formatting: Use the IEEE conference template
  • Anonymization: Remove all author information for blind review
  • Language: English only
  • Deadline: April 1, 2026, 11:59 PM EST
  • Originality: Submitted papers must be original and not previously published

SUBMISSION FORM

After Submission

Once you submit your paper, you will receive an immediate confirmation email with your submission ID. The review process typically takes 4-6 weeks. You will be notified of the decision via email. If you need to update your submission before the deadline, please contact us at submissions@conference2026.org
