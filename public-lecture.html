Public Lecture

The Future of AI: Ethics and Innovation
An exploration of how artificial intelligence is reshaping our world and the ethical considerations we must address as we advance.

DateApril 15, 2026
Time2:00 PM - 4:00 PM EST
VenueGrand Hall, Convention Center
Photo of the speaker

Prof. Anjali Bose

Title: Chair, AI Ethics Group

Details: Globally recognized researcher in AI fairness, accountability and governance. Prof. Bose leads multidisciplinary teams exploring ethical AI policy and responsible innovation.

Lecture Description

This talk will cover the current state of AI ethics research, practical cases of deploying AI systems responsibly, and strategies for innovation that embed ethical considerations from ideation through deployment.
