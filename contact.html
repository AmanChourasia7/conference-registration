Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the conference?

Visit our Registration page to complete your registration online. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately.

What is the refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Between 30-14 days, a 50% refund is provided. No refunds within 14 days of the event.

Are group discounts available?

Yes! We offer discounts for groups of 5 or more. Contact us at registration@techconference2026.com for details.

Can I transfer my ticket?

Yes, tickets are transferable. Contact our registration team with the new attendee's information at least 7 days before the event.

What is included in my registration?

Your ticket includes access to all keynotes, sessions, workshops, networking events, conference materials, and meals during the event.

How do I become a sponsor?

We offer various sponsorship packages. Email us at sponsors@techconference2026.com for our sponsorship prospectus.
